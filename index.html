About Arunima Das

Meet the passionate curator of Assamese culinary heritage, dedicated to preserving and sharing the authentic flavors of her homeland

A Journey Through Flavors

Arunima Das was born and raised in Assam, surrounded by the delicious aromas and flavors of Assamese cuisine. She learned cooking from her mother.discovering that food is about more than just taste ‚Äî it's about nourishing the soul and preserving culture.

With over 5 years of experience in documenting and recreating traditional recipes, Arunima has become a passionate advocate for authentic Assamese cooking. She believes that every dish tells a story - of seasons, celebrations, healing, and the deep connection between food and community.

Through this platform, she shares not just recipes, but the wisdom, traditions, and love that make Assamese cuisine truly special. Her mission is to ensure that these precious culinary treasures are passed down to future generations, keeping the flavors of Assam alive in kitchens around the world.
